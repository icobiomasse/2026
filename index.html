About the Conference

A Premier Platform for Biomass Innovation

The 6th International Conference on Biomass Utilization & Sustainable Energy (ICoBiomasSE 2026) is a prestigious global platform dedicated to advancing circular bioeconomy through cutting-edge biomass research, innovative conversion technologies, and sustainable energy transitions. This conference brings together leading researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and postgraduate students to share groundbreaking discoveries, foster international collaborations, and accelerate the decarbonisation of our global economy.

With publication opportunities in SCOPUS-indexed proceedings, comprehensive peer-review processes, and recognition from prestigious engineering bodies, ICoBiomasSE 2026 stands as a beacon of academic excellence and innovation in biomass utilization and renewable energy systems.
